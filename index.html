Safety Tips

  • Before Dyeing: Ask customer to perform patch test 48 hours before application
  • During Dyeing: Follow instructions precisely, use proper tools
  • After Dyeing: Rinse thoroughly, use color-safe shampoo, wait 48 hours before washing

Oxygen Levels (Developer) / مستويات الأكسجين

Oxygen is the substance mixed with dye to activate color and determine coloring strength.

10 Volume (3%)

Semi-permanent dyes and light coloring. Gentle, least damaging.

20 Volume (6%)

Permanent dyes and light lightening. Balanced and suitable for natural hair.

30 Volume (9%)

Permanent dyes and medium lightening. Strong coloring power.

40 Volume (12%)

Strong lightening and full gray coverage. Use with caution.

Concentrations and Their Uses / التركيزات واستخداماتها

Concentration refers to the amount of active dye in the product.

100% - Very High Concentration Permanent

Full gray coverage, very strong coloring. Deep color, very long-lasting (8+ weeks).

80-90% - Medium-High Concentration Permanent

Excellent gray coverage, strong coloring. Deep color, long-lasting (6-8 weeks).

30-40% - Semi-Permanent Semi-Perm

Gentle coloring, added shine. Light color, short-lasting (2-4 weeks).
